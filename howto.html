How to Use the Trainer

This app is designed to help you master the final two steps of the CFOP method: OLL and PLL. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to read your cube and use the AI detection tools.

The Basics (CFOP)

Before using this trainer, you should have completed the first two steps of the CFOP solving method:

  • Step 1: The Cross. A red cross on the bottom of the cube.
  • Step 2: F2L (First Two Layers). The bottom two layers are completely solved.
Step 3

OLL Orientation of the Last Layer

Your goal in OLL is to make the entire top face one solid color (usually orange), ignoring the side colors for now. There are 57 different ways the orange pieces can land.

How to use the OLL tool:

  1. Hold your cube so the unsolved face (Orange) is pointing straight up at the ceiling.
  2. Map the top face: Look at your cube and tap the squares in the center 3x3 grid on the app to match the exact orange sticker layout you see.
  3. Map the sides: Look at the top row of stickers on the sides of your cube (front, back, left, right). Tap the outer strips on the app to indicate which of those side stickers are orange.
  4. Tap DETECT MATCH. The AI will instantly find your exact pattern out of the 57 possibilities.
  5. Follow the Hold Instructions: Look at the "Correct Angle" picture in the app. Rotate your physical cube in your hands until it matches that angle perfectly, then execute the algorithm!
Step 4

PLL Permutation of the Last Layer

Your entire top face is now orange, but the side colors are mixed up. In PLL, you slide those pieces around until the cube is solved. There are 21 different PLL algorithms.

How to use the PLL tool:

  1. Identify the swaps: Look at the top layer of your cube from the side. Figure out which specific pieces need to trade places to match the bottom two layers.
  2. Draw the arrows: On the trainer's PLL tab, tap one square in the center grid, then tap its destination square to draw an arrow (or simply tap and drag between them).
    • Swaps: If two pieces trade places with each other, draw one line between them. It will automatically become a 2-way swap arrow.
    • Cycles: If 3 or 4 pieces move in a circle, trace their exact path (e.g., tap A then B, tap B then C).
  3. Mistake? Draw the exact same arrow again to delete it, or hit the "Reset Pattern" button to clear the board.
  4. Tap DETECT MATCH. The AI will calculate the PLL case regardless of how you are holding the cube. Align to the correct angle and execute the algorithm to solve it!

App Features & Pro Tips

  • Full Databases: Scroll down on either the OLL or PLL tab to expand the "Full List" dropdowns. You can search by shape or browse every algorithm in the database.
  • Copy Algorithms: Found an algorithm you like? Hit the "COPY" button next to it to easily save it to your notes.
  • Device Friendly: This trainer is built to work seamlessly on both your desktop monitor and as a mobile web app on your phone!
